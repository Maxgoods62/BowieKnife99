Enable mod
Turn the random ramming attacks on or off.
Show debug HUD
Show on-screen debug lines (ram start, pick counts, SMS status). Off for normal play.
Behavior
Attack chance
Probability an attack triggers on each check (0 = never, 1 = every check).
Check interval (s)
Seconds between attack checks. Lower = attacks happen more often.
Cooldown (checks)
How many check intervals to wait after an attack before another can trigger.
Pursuit timeout (s)
How long Bowie chases before giving up if he never lands a hit.
Max attacker distance (m)
Only cars within this distance behind you can be chosen (they still need ~18 m of runway).
Max height difference (m)
Skip cars whose elevation differs from yours by more than this — avoids cars on a bridge above or a road below.
Attack from behind only
On: only cars behind you are chosen, so the attacker chases up from the rear (natural-looking). Off (default): any nearby car can attack, including oncoming/side traffic.
Bump speed boost (m/s)
When Bowie gets close he surges forward by this much (m/s) to bump you. 0 = no surge, just aggressive driving.
Audio
Play taunt sound on ram
Play a voice line every time Bowie rams your car.
